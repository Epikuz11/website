DayZ Wiki
DayZ is a multiplayer-only survival video game developed and published by Bohemia Interactive. It is the standalone successor of the mod of the same name for the game ARMA 2. Following a five-year long early access period for Windows, the game was officially released into Beta in December 2018, and was released for the Xbox One and PlayStation 4 in 2019.

Current Stable version: 1.2
Current Experimental (PC/Xbox) version: 1.2
